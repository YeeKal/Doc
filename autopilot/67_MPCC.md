## MPCC

Mathematical Program with Complementarity Constraints (MPCC)

complementarity constraints enforcing nonnegativity and
orthogonality of Q(x) and P(x)

$$0\leq Q(x)\perp P(x) \geq 0 $$

$$0\leq Q(x), Q(x)\perp P(x) = 0, P(x) \geq 0 $$
